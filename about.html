About us

Vision

SAHAT Center’s target is to have Somaliland participate in the 2028 Olympic games and beyond.

Mission

SAHAT Center wishes to be an academy that educates and trains future athletes who can compete on the international stage, and model citizens who can positively contribute to society. .
